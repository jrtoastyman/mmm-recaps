VCU chaos

Day one delivered exactly what it promised: false hope for half the field and early elimination for everyone who took Duke going deep. Four double-digit seeds decided they didn't get the memo about chalk.

The East region went mostly according to script — emphasis on "mostly." If your bracket survived today, congratulations. You are about to find out what Friday has in store for it.

The standout performance of the day belongs to VCU, who took Wisconsin to overtime in what can only be described as a deeply personal attack on anyone who needed Wisconsin in the second round. The Rams won by three. Wisconsin fans have requested privacy at this time.

At least one bracket submitted by someone named "Cinderella Believer" is quietly sitting in the top 20. We'll see how that ages.
